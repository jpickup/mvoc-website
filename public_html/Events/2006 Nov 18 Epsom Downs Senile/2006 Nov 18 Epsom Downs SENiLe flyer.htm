SENiLe
(South East Night League)
EPSOM DOWNS
18 November 2006

Last chance to hone your Night O skills before the Southern Night Championships
at OldHouse Warren on 25 November
 

Terrain: Epsom Downs is an area of fast runnable downland with small areas of woodland. The Navy course will also take in Epsom Downs golf course (first time use). There are fine views even at night!
 
Parking: At Grid Reference TQ224584 on the B290 between Epsom and Tattenham Corner (the road which goes in front of the Epsom Race Course Grandstand)
 
Map: 1:10,000 5m contour interval : printed on waterproof paper (bring your own bag if required)
 
Registration: 5:00pm to 6:00pm
 
Starts: 5:30pm to 6:30pm  Courses close 8:00pm
 
Fees:  £5.00 Seniors; £2.00 Juniors and Students
 
Punching: The event will be using SportIdent electronic punching (hire charge £1)
 
Courses: Ochre (2.0km), Olive (4.0Km), Navy (7.0km)
 
Facilities: Very limited. No toilets
Nearest A&E Unit at Epsom General Hospital
 
Dogs: Welcome under control
 
Officials: Organiser and Planner : Neil Lewer, MV (07878 722256)
Controller: TBA

MV website: www.mvoc.org
 
Safety: Whistles recommended. The Navy course involves several road crossings - please take care!
 
Please email the organiser if you are hoping to come, with a note of your intended course in the subject field. This places you under no obligation, but will help us estimate the number of maps likely to be required
