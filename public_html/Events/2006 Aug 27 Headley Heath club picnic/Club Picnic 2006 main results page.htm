CLUB PICNIC
HEADLEY HEATH
27 AUGUST 2006

Thanks to everyone who came to the 40th Birthday Club Picnic Event.

Those who didn't make it missed out on spot prizes, a cake from last year's winner Maggie Stedman (celebrating our 40th and her own 70th birthdays), the first outing of the MV Club Banner, beautiful weather and a lot of fun.

Planners Sandra and Tony particularly want to thank:

- Those Moles who helped collect controls after their runs: George Engelhardt, Keith Masson, Ted McDonald, Chris Starnes, Graham Sutton

- Mike Elliot for excellent computing support both before and after his run

- Those who brought food to share
