REGIONAL EVENT, SOUTH ASHDOWN

12 FEBRUARY 2006

Terrain: Undulating open moorland with stream valleys
Parking: On Pippingford Park, adjoining the northern edge of the competition area. Entrance off the A22 south of Wych Cross at TQ 437303
Map: By Mike Elliot. Map scale: 1:10,000 and 1:15,000 (to be confirmed)
Courses Regional: JM/W 1-4, JM5 L/M/S, JW5 L/S, M/W 21 L/S/V/N, M/W 35-75 L/S
Colour-coded: White, Yellow, Orange, Light Green, Red
No string course
Punching: Sportident on all courses
SI cards available for hire at £1.00
Pre-entry for regional courses: Online entry (preferred): http://www.fabian4.co.uk
Postal entry: On Standard Entry Form (with two 9" x 6" SAEs if you require printed details and/or results) to Mike Bolton, 17 Kemp Court, Church Place, Brighton, East Sussex, BN2 5JU. Cheques made payable to "Mole Valley Orienteering Club"
Enquiries: Mike Bolton, address as above, or phone 01273 670732, email mike.bolton@bromcom.com
Fees:  Regional Seniors: (online by 31st January or by post, postmarked no later than 25th January): £9:00. Late entries and EOD (subject to map availability): £10.00
Regional Juniors: (online by 31st January or by post, postmarked no later than 25th January): £3:00. Late entries and EOD (subject to map availability): £3.50
Colour-coded seniors: (EOD only): £4.00
Colour-coded juniors: (EOD only): £1.00
Parking: £1 per car
Times: Registration 9:30 -12:00
Starts 10:00 - 12:30
Courses close 14:30
Officials: Organisers: Mike Bolton and Peter Heywood
Planner: Nick Green
Controller: Simon Maytum (WCOC)
Facilities: Toilets, Squash, First Aid
Dogs: Welcome, on a lead
Safety: South Ashdown is an exposed area that needs to be treated with caution in bad weather. Whistles will be compulsory. Cagoules may be required. In extreme conditions check whether the event has been cancelled by going to the Mole Valley Website (http://www.mvoc.org) or phoning 01293-862299.
Conditions Competitors take part at their own risk.
Entry details will be stored in a computer. Completion of an entry form implies your acceptance of this.
In the unlikely event of cancellation, we may retain all or part of the entry fee to cover costs already incurred.
MV website:  www.mvoc.org
