Pre-event information: flyer

We hope you enjoyed the Try Orienteering event in Morden Hall Park.

If you would like to try some more orienteering, then you might like to put the following dates in your diary:

18 July at Ashtead Park

25 September at Horton Country Park

9 October at Box Hill

23 October at Reigate Priory

All these events are Try Orienteering events, and you are welcome to come along and get some more experience.

We will put information about these events on our home page as the dates get closer.

In the mean time, if you have any questions at all please do email info@mvoc.org and we will do our best to answer them.

If you'd like to give us any feedback about trying orienteering, we would love to hear that too!
