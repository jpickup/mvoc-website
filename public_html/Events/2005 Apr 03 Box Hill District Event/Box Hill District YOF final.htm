The team will be made up as follows:

Years 5 & 6:    4 boys and 4 girls  (max)  running White
Years 7 & 8:    4 boys and 4 girls  (max)  running Yellow
Years 9, 10 & 11:    4 boys and 4 girls  (max)  running Orange
