MOLE VALLEY ORIENTEERING CLUB

(Affiliated to the British Orienteering Federation)

DISTRICT (COLOUR CODED) EVENT

Balcombe – Near Crawley

Sunday 28th March 2004

 

Venue
Balcombe is a combination of woodland with large rock features and open countryside

Car Park
Signposted from the B2036 in centre of Balcombe village at Grid ref. TQ. Car parking will be tight, please share cars if at all possible. Later arrivals may be directed to an overflow car park with half a mail walk to registration

Map
1:10000 by Mike Elliot updated Winter 2002; contour interval 5m

Registration
09.30 - 11.30 Entry on the day only.

Starts
10.00 - 12.00  Courses close 14:30

Courses & Punching
White to Brown. Free string course. The event will be using Sportident electronic punching. (hire charge £1)

Fees
Seniors £4.50.  Juniors £2.00

Facilities
Squash, toilets, dogs welcome providing kept on a lead at all times.
