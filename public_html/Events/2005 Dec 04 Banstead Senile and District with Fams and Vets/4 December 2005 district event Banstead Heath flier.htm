DISTRICT EVENT, BANSTEAD HEATH

04 DECEMBER 2005
 

 
   
Terrain: Banstead Heath - mixed woodland with many intricate features and open grassland with patches of gorse
Brown and Blue courses cross a fairly busy B road twice - crossing points will be marshalled.
Parking: Street parking on New Road and Mill Road between Grid Refs TQ231557 and TQ241557, on Landranger sheet 187 / Explorer sheet 146. 
Please share transport where possible as space is very limited, and be aware that you may be some distance from Registration. 
Please follow directions from marshals and respect use by non-orienteers and local residents, including horse-riders and dog-walkers.
Map: New, surveyed and drawn by Mike Elliot January 2005.  Scale 1:10,000,  Contour Interval 5m
Maps will be overprinted but unbagged - please bring your own bag for size A4 or larger
Registration: 9:30am – 12:30pm in Tadworth Cricket Club pavilion, Grid Ref: TQ239557 north side of Mill Road
Starts: 10:00am – 12:30pm  
Courses close 2:00pm
Fees:  BOF & MV Seniors £5.00; Non-BOF £6.00; Juniors/Students £2.00 
Punching: The event will be using SportIdent electronic punching (hire charge £1.00).
Courses:  Brown, Blue, Green, Light Green, Orange and Yellow (no Red)
Sorry no string course or White course
SE Veterans Championships: First M40+ finisher on the Blue course and first W40+ finisher on the Green course running for a SEOA affiliated club. 
Holders Vince Joyce SO and Julianna Grant MV.
Families competition: Handicap competition for related groups of four.  Please ask for the special entry form at Registration, run the courses of your choice and we will do the rest.
Facilities: In Tadworth CC pavilion – Registration, Download, First Aid Box, Toilets, changing facilities, Squash.
The Cricket Club may also be providing Snack facilities (tea, coffee, bacon butties etc.)
Nearest A&E unit at either Epsom General Hospital or East Surrey Hospital, Redhill.
Dogs:  Under control at all times.  Be aware that the Heath is used by dog-walkers and horse-riders.
Officials: Planner: Graham Sutton
Organiser: Simon Ling – 01372 813047 simonATsimonling.org.uk
Controller: Please note there is no external controller, but courses have been checked by P Gristwood, MV, and other club members
MV website:  www.mvoc.org
