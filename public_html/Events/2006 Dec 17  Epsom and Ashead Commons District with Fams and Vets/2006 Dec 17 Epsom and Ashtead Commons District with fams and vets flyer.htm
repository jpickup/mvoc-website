DISTRICT EVENT
EPSOM AND ASHTEAD COMMONS
17 DECEMBER 2006
INCLUDING SOUTH EAST VETERANS CHAMPIONSHIP
AND FAMILIES COMPETITION

Terrain: Mainly fairly level deciduous woodland with many clearings, glades etc. Extensive foot and bridle path network. Can be very muddy in wet weather.
 
Parking: At Ashtead rail station car park GR TQ180590. The car park is free on Sundays but is only available on a first-come first-served basis and is also open to the public. Please fill up from far end of car park using marked spaces. Street parking locally available for overflow. Use public transport (rail to Ashtead) if possible.
 
Map: 1:10,000 by Mike Elliot, 2006
Maps will not be bagged - please bring your own bag
 
Registration: 9.30am to 12.00pm in nearby scout hut
(Download and toilets are also here)
 
Starts: 10.00am to 12.30pm       Courses close 2.00pm
 
Fees:  £7 Seniors; £4 Juniors and Students
Discount of £2 for BOF members - bring your membership card
 
Punching: The event will be using SportIdent electronic punching (hire charge £1)
 
Courses: Brown, Blue, Green, Light Green, Red, Orange, Yellow, White. Sorry, no string course
 
Control Descriptions: Control descriptions will be on the map. There will be no loose control descriptions
 
SE Veterans Championships: First M40+ finisher on the Blue course and first W40+ finisher on the Green course running for a SEOA affiliated club.
Current holders are Mike Murray (SLOW) and Jackie Chapman (SLOW)
 
Families Competition: Handicap competition for related groups of four. Please ask for special entry form at Registration, run the courses of your choice and we will do the rest. Current holders Chapman/Hooper family (SO)
 
Facilities: Nearest A&E Unit at Epsom General Hospital
 
Dogs: Welcome under control
 
Officials: Planner: Simon Ling (MV)
Organiser: Ted McDonald (MV)
Controller: Andrew Evans (DFOK)

MV website: www.mvoc.org
