HORTON NORTH

20 SEPTEMBER 2003

 

A note from the organiser
We had a beautiful day for Horton Country Park and judging by the people I talked to the morning’s orienteering was first rate.  

Well-done Christopher Schofield for winning the yellow course. Ian and Adam Bloxsome are now heading up the yellow league after two events. The league is based on your best three runs so if you have a problem on one you can always make it up on the next event.  

It looks like the Bloxsomes’ are making this their own series!  

Special thanks to all the helpers who gave up their Saturday morning to get the second event in the Saturday Series up and running (pun intended).  

I look forward to seeing you all on the 4 October at Nonsuch Park from 10.am

 Hugh Walker (Mole Valley Orienteering Club)
