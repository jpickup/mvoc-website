HORTON SOUTH

17 OCTOBER 2003

 

A note from the organiser
The fourth and final event in Mole Valley Orienteering Club Saturday Series introduced electronic punching, produced a record turn out and gave us the winners of the three leagues. Not bad for a Saturday in October.

Over 57 runners enjoyed the event, with a record turn out in every class. 40 of these new to the Series! The electronic punching was a great success, allowing competitors a stressless start, easy punching (especially in the cold) and result times & splits.

Well done Jeff Jones, Neil Lewis and Andriy Malchyk for winning their events, all in great times (It‘s a bit frightening to see the winner of Light Green only three minutes behind the winner of the Yellow course).

Also well done the MacDonald family, Matt Goddard and Justin Farhall. Bad luck for Samantha Martin whose mis-punch on the final day lost her the second place on the Orange League.

 Hugh Walker (Mole Valley Orienteering Club)
