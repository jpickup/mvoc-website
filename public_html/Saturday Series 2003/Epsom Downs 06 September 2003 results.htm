EPSOM DOWNS

06 SEPTEMBER 2003

 

A note from the organiser
Thanks to everyone who turned up. After a slightly rearranged start, which did not delay anything, the morning went very well. A well thought out course did not cause any major problems and everyone I spoke enjoyed their run.

Well done to Tom Pollard, Tom Snape, Matt Goddard and Ian Bloxsome for heading up the tables – lets see if they can maintain their lead?

Special thanks to all the helpers who gave up their Saturday morning to get the first event of the series off to a flying start.

I look forward to seeing you all on the 20 September at Horton Country Park at the earlier time of 10.am

Hugh Walker (Mole Valley Orienteering Club)
