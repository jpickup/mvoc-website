NONSUCH PARK

04 OCTOBER 2003

 

A note from the organiser
Autumn came to Nonsuch Park but it did not dampen a really enjoyable morning. The courses made full use of the wide opens spaces and interesting features in the park and everyone enjoyed their run.

Special thanks to all the helpers who gave up their Saturday morning to make the third event in the Saturday Series a success.

I look forward to seeing you all on the 18 October at Horton Country Park from 10.am – start times will be between 10.30 and 11.30.

Hugh Walker (Mole Valley Orienteering Club)
