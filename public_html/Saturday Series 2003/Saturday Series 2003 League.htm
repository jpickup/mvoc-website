SATURDAY SERIES 2003 
LEAGUE RESULTS

 

League Points explained
Points are awarded at each of the four events in the series, contributing to an overall league score. The best three of four events count for the final league results, so your worst score will be dropped if you do all four events. Those score marked with an asterisk (*) have been dropped.

The top finishers on Yellow, Orange and Light Green courses are given 100 points. If the second finisher is within 30 seconds of the winner they get 100 points too. The points then decrease as you get further away from the winner.
