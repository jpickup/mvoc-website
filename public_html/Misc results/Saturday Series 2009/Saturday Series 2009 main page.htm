The Come And Try Orienteering League
The League will be put together using a scoring system worked out on the basis of your position in each one of the three Come And Try Orienteering events you take part in plus some other orienteering events. 

You can see the positions after the first two events and the full list of events you can compete in for League points here.


These CAT-O events were designed as an introduction to orienteering. If you have any comments or questions either about these events or about orienteering in general please email us. For example you might like to contact us to:

- ask about other orienteering events in your area

- find out more about our permanent orienteering courses, in places like Redhill Common and Buchan Country Park
