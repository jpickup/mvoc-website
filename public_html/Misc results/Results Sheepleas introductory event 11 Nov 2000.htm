RESULTS OF SHEEPLEAS INTRODUCTORY EVENT

 11 NOVEMBER 2000

Planners Comments

Thanks for coming along on what was a fairly wild morning, and I hope you enjoyed your courses.

Thanks also for comments made during and after the event, which were most helpful, and a big thank you to those Mole Valley members who helped out, or offered help.

The next local MV event is at Ranmore Common near Dorking, on 7 January. If you can’t wait that long, Guildford Orienteers have an event at St Martha’s, Guildford, on 26 November.

Enjoy your orienteering.                                                                                                    Jon Coles
