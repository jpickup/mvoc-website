RESULTS OF NORBURY PARK LIMITED COLOUR CODED EVENT

03 SEPTEMBER 2000

Sun blessed Norbury Park put on its best face for this event and most seemed to enjoy their course. The area provides an excellent introduction to orienteering - with a good path network, open fields and forest, and good views across the Mole. For the old hands, the scarp slope, yews and mini-hide always prove a challenge. How many of you on the Green (or should that have been Turquoise?) noticed a route choice from 10-11 dropping down to the path and avoiding 25m of climb? Thanks to all the Moles who helped and to Mike Elliot for his controlling.


Tony Burton
(Planner & Organiser)
