Thanks to everyone who came to the events. We hope you had a good time.

These events were designed as an introduction to orienteering. If you have any comments or questions either about these events or about orienteering in general please email us. For example you might like to contact us to:

- comment on the courses, including how you enjoyed the experience of orienteering

- ask about other orienteering events in your area

- find out more about our permanent orienteering courses, like the ones at Redhill Common and Oaks Park
