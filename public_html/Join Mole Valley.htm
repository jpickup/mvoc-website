JOIN MOLE VALLEY

 

To join Mole Valley use the online form at the British Orienteering web site – https://www.britishorienteering.org.uk/join_online Select 'MV' (for Mole Valley) as your club and it will default to 'SEOA' as your Association (South East Orienteering Association).

By becoming a member you qualify for free entry to our local evening events; access to training activities and advice; copies of newsletters and the club magazine; and the opportunity to have your say in the way the club is run at the Annual General Meeting and via the Committee.

By joining British Orienteering you benefit from their Civil Liability Insurance. Membership also qualifies you for a discount on entry fees for other clubs’ events – usually £2 for Seniors and £1 for Juniors – so it soon pays for itself.

Anyone joining after 1 November in any year will get membership until 31 December the following year.

Subscription renewal notifications will be sent out to existing members by British Orienteering with a view to getting new membership cards to people before 1 January each year.

Notes

Juniors are aged 20 or under on 1 January of the year they join or renew.

The Supporter membership class is designed for people who no longer orienteer but want to stay in touch with MV or people who are members of another orienteering club but who also support MV by helping the club to put on events. Supporters are not members of British Orienteering and do not get the benefits of such membership (unless they belong via another club).

If you would like to apply to join Mole Valley as a Supporter or have any other questions regarding membership, please contact the membership secretary.
