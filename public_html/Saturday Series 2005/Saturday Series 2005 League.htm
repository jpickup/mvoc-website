LEAGUE RESULTS

 

League Points explained
Points are awarded at each of the four events in the series, contributing to an overall league score. The best three of four events count for the final league results, so your worst score will be dropped if you take part in all four events. When the final scores are calculated after the fourth event, the dropped score will be marked with an asterisk (*).

The top finishers on both the 2k and 3k courses are given twenty points. Points are then awarded depending on how far behind the first placed person you are. So if you were second on your course you will get 19 points, if you were third, 18 points and so on.

The nineteenth placed competitor and any after then get 2 points. Anyone who took part but was disqualified or did not complete a course, gets 1 point.
